Still on the fence? Let's clear that up

IPTV stands for Internet Protocol Television, which means it delivers television content over the internet rather than through traditional broadcast, cable, or satellite methods. Instead of a physical cable box or satellite dish, you simply stream your favorite channels directly to your connected devices.

Our IPTV service is highly flexible and works on a wide variety of devices. You can stream seamlessly on Smart TVs, Android boxes, Amazon Fire Sticks, Roku devices, mobile phones, and tablets. All you need is our recommended app or a compatible IPTV player to get started.

To ensure the best viewing experience without buffering, your required internet speed depends on the broadcast quality:

  • Standard Definition (SD): Minimum of 3 Mbps.
  • High Definition (HD): Minimum of 5 Mbps.
  • Ultra High Definition (4K): Minimum of 25 Mbps or higher for optimal performance.
